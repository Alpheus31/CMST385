Sin Eater is about a group of immortals that have been tasked with the fate of humanity. After witnessing
the fall of the Tower of Babel, fourteen men and women are greeted by an archangel that commands them
to go forth and absoulve the sins of man. These fourteen form seven tribes and secretly
watch man from the shadows.
