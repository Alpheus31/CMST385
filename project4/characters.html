Characters

Gin

Gin is the last member of the Tamura clan. Little is known about her past.
Her clan was responsible for the security of a very important artifact.br Her clan was wiped out and the artifact was lost. Her weapon of choice is a katana.

Marcus

Marcus is of roman birth. He has served as both Caesar and has also been a pauper.
Marcus is of the Greed clan. They have made their home in Pennsylvania.
He has become disillusioned with the way the clan does business and have fallen out
of favor with his father. Marcus prefers the roman gladius.

Anne

Anne is a mystery. She is currently in New Oreleans where she has Gin and Marcus held captive.
She is in the employ of the Mistress of Lust.
