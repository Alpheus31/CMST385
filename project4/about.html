Sin Eater Comics is a story that started out as a joke among friends.
Two friends were chatting about the roman legions and how things worked with them.
At one point, the act of eating another person's sins was added to the conversation.
People who are able to do this are called Sin Eaters. After that all it took was to
add the details.
